Exploders VR



Embark on a high-stakes mission of defusing bombs in Virtual Reality with Exploders. Test your time management and quick decision-making skills as you race against the clock to locate and defuse explosive devices. Beware of the consequences of a wrong wire cut! Can you defuse every bomb in time and save the city?

Game Features :

  • Sci-fi Lab Exploration: Dive into the immersive world of a sci-fi lab, equipped with advanced measuring instruments like ammeters and voltmeters.
  • Various Bomb Types: Encounter different bomb types (5V, 9V, 12V) and master the art of defusing each one within the given time.
  • Guidebook and Explosive Parameters: Study the guidebook and handle the bombs while considering various explosive parameters.
