FOAR Elements

An Augmented Reality game build with Niantic ARDK sdk.

FOAR Elements, a shared multiplayer experience game, based on the theme that the entire universe is created with FIVE different elements (AIR, WATER, SKY, EARTH, FIRE), in Indian culture we refer to these as "Panchamahabhutas". Players have to explore around the world to capture different powers from natural sources and remove the negative energy by battling with enemies. Players can have Co-Op mode in battle by creating a shared multiplayer experience.

Tool Used:
1.) Unity 3D
2.) C#
3.) Niantic ARDK
